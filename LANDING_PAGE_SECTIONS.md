## Color Scheme

- **Primary**: `#002400` (Dark Green) - Headers, text, dark sections
- **Secondary**: `#00dd78` (Bright Green) - CTAs, accents, highlights
- **Background**: `#F2FFF2` (Light Green) - Page background
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Cards, sections
- **Light Green**: `#00dd781a` (20% opacity) - Section backgrounds

## Typography

- **Font Family**: Mona Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: Bold, 24px-56px depending on level
- **Body**: Regular, 14px-18px
- **CTAs**: Semibold, 16px-18px

## Spacing

- **Section Padding**: 48px-96px (responsive)
- **Card Gaps**: 24px-32px
- **Element Spacing**: 16px-24px
- **Max Width**: 1280px (7xl container)

## Responsive Breakpoints

- **Mobile**: < 640px (1 column)
- **Tablet**: 640px-1024px (2 columns)
- **Desktop**: > 1024px (3-4 columns)

## Interactive Elements

### Buttons
- Primary: Secondary color background, primary text
- Secondary: Primary color background, white text
- Outline: White background, border, primary text
- Hover: Slight color change + shadow increase
- Active: Scale down (0.95)

### Cards
- Border: 2px solid gray-100
- Hover: Border changes to secondary/50, shadow increases
- Rounded: 16px (rounded-2xl)
- Padding: 24px-32px

### Links
- Default: Primary/70 color
- Hover: Secondary color
- Transition: 150ms ease
